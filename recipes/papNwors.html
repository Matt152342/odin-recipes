Pap and words recipe

Description

This recipe is to guide you to making lovely wors and wonderful soft pap.

Ingredients

  • Wors
  • Maize meal
  • Water
  • Boerie Relish

Steps

  1. Bring 1 litre of water and 12.5ml of salt to a boil.
  2. Stir 750ml maize meal.
  3. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 to 30 minutes.
  4. Grill or pan-fry wors over medium heat for 5 minutes on each side.
  5. Saute finely chopped onion in oil and chapped tomatoes, garlic and herbs.
  6. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
