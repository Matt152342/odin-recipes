Hot Dogs

Description

Basic hot dog recipe. Simple, but delicious.

Ingredients

  • Smoke russian sauseges
  • Sesame seeds
  • Buns of your choice
  • Garlic mayo
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Fry two smoked russian sauseges till golden
  2. Brush buns with butter and dip in black sesame seeds
  3. Top your hotdog with garlic mayo and mustard
  4. Enjoy
