Lasagna Recipe

Description

This recipe is to guide you through the process of making wonderful lasagna yourself. Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Aromatics: Onion and garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Seasoning of choice
  • Cheese (ricotta and parmesan)
  • Eggs
  • Pasta
  • Bechamel sauce

Steps

  1. Brown your meat with garlic and onions
  2. Mix ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese and 1 beaten egg
  3. Boil your chosen pasta until firm to bite
  4. Preheat oven to 190 degrees (celsius)
  5. Spread thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of chosen baking dish
  6. Place a layer of pasta, followed by a layer of cheese
  7. Repeat last two steps until satisfied and filled the baking dish
  8. Cover with foil and bake
  9. Uncover foil and brown
  10. Let the lasagna rest for another 15-30 minutes
